About Me

A young professional with an eye for design and a deep understanding of the human experience.

I care deeply about inclusivity, accessibility, and growth. I approach design from a holistic perspective - meaning I take into account every part of the whole and I consider all different types of the human experiences.

I understand user's psychological, mental and social needs. My degree in Anthropology allows me to take this holistic approach because of my understanding of human culture(s), which means I can take on the perspective of all different types of users and their motivations.
